Make sure you're loading all the necessary modules in the kernel (2.6.x)


/sbin/modprobe ip_conntrack
/sbin/modprobe ip_gre
/sbin/modprobe ip_conntrack_pptp
/sbin/modprobe ip_nat_pptp
