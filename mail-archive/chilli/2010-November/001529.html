We are pleased to release CoovaRADIUS 1.0.1 for Ubuntu, CentOS,
openSUSE, Windows, MacOSX, VMware, LiveCD, Install/Preload CD, or
anywhere else Java runs!

http://coova-radius.s3.amazonaws.com/CoovaRADIUS-1.0.1.pdf
